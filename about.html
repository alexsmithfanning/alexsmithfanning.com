About

General information

I was born June 5th, 2000 in Gardner, Kansas to my mom Kellie Smith and my father Timothy Fanning (hence the last name Smith-Fanning). I've been in Gardner all my life, and I hope to keep it that way. My father left before I was born and is now living somewhere in Arizona, so its just me, my mom, and my cat living together. While I was growing up I never really got to do much because we didn't really have a lot of money, but we were far from poor. In 2007 I went to Florida and got to have my first glimpse of the ocean and everything in it. From that point on I loved the ocean—and I still do. In 2010 my mom took me to Jamaica where I once again got to experice the ocean. That was the last time I got to travel, because in 2009 my grandmother died and because of some extenuating circumstances we had to move out. Since that point we have been living just below the poverty line, paycheck to paycheck.
